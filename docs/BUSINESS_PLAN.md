# WheelWill Productions — Business Plan

> *Stories in Motion*

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**WheelWill Productions** is a film and video production company founded in 2026, 
based in Los Angeles, CA. We specialize in creating high-quality visual content 
including short films, documentaries, commercials, music videos, and corporate 
video for brands, artists, and organizations.

Our mission is to craft visually compelling stories that move audiences — from 
concept to screen. We combine cinematic storytelling with professional production 
quality to deliver content that resonates.

## 3. Services

### Primary Revenue Streams
1. **Commercial Production** — Brand films, TV spots, digital ads
2. **Music Video Production** — For independent and signed artists
3. **Short Film Production** — Narrative short-form cinema
4. **Documentary Production** — Non-fiction storytelling
5. **Corporate Video** — Training, event coverage, internal comms
6. **Post-Production Services** — Editing, color grading, sound design, VFX

### Pricing Model
| Service              | Starting Price | Notes                        |
|----------------------|----------------|------------------------------|
| Short Film           | $5,000         | Depends on scope & duration  |
| Commercial (30s)     | $3,000         | Concept through delivery     |
| Music Video          | $2,500         | Basic package                |
| Documentary          | $8,000         | Depends on length & travel   |
| Corporate Video      | $2,000         | Per finished minute varies   |
| Post-Production Only | $500/day       | Editing, color, sound        |

*All pricing is customizable based on project scope.*

## 4. Target Market

### Primary Audiences
- **Small to mid-size businesses** needing brand video content
- **Independent musicians & artists** needing music videos
- **Startups** launching products or building brand awareness
- **Nonprofits** wanting documentary-style storytelling
- **Agencies** outsourcing video production

- 91% of businesses use video as a marketing tool
- Short-form video demand growing 30%+ annually

## 9. Growth Roadmap

### Year 1 — Foundation
- Establish brand and online presence
- Complete 25+ client projects
- Build portfolio with 3-4 standout pieces
- Build freelance network

### Year 2 — Expansion
- Hire first full-time team member
- Expand into adjacent markets (e.g., podcast video, live events)
- Submit to film festivals
- Revenue target: $250,000

### Year 3 — Scale
- Studio space or dedicated office
- In-house post-production team
- National/international client base
- Revenue target: $500,000
