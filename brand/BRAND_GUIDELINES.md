# WheelWill — Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Overview

**Company Name:** WheelWill  
**Industry:** Film & Video Production  
**Founded:** 2026  
**Tagline:** *"Stories in Motion"*

### Mission
To craft visually compelling stories that move audiences — from concept to screen.

### Vision
To become a recognized name in independent film and commercial video production, known for creative storytelling, technical excellence, and authentic narratives.

### Core Values
- **Creativity** — Bold ideas, original perspectives
- **Craftsmanship** — Excellence in every frame
- **Collaboration** — Great stories are built together
- **Integrity** — Honest storytelling, ethical business

---

## 2. Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- The WheelWill logo features a stylized wheel motif representing motion and progress
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the "W" on all sides
- Minimum size: 32px height for digital, 0.5" for print

### Logo Variations
| Variant        | Usage                              |
|----------------|-------------------------------------|
| Full color     | Default on light backgrounds        |
| White          | On dark backgrounds or video overlays|
| Monochrome     | Print materials, watermarks         |
| Icon only      | Social media avatars, favicons      |

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not change the logo colors
- Do not place on busy backgrounds without a container
- Do not add drop shadows or effects

## 4. Typography

### Primary Typeface
**Font:** `Inter` (Google Fonts)  
- Headings: Inter Bold (700)  
- Subheadings: Inter SemiBold (600)  
- Body: Inter Regular (400)  
- Captions: Inter Light (300)

### Secondary Typeface (for cinematic/creative use)
**Font:** `Playfair Display`  
- Used for titles, hero sections, and editorial content

### Type Scale
| Element     | Size   | Weight | Line Height |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------------|
| H1          | 48px   | 700    | 1.2         |
| H2          | 36px   | 700    | 1.3         |
| H3          | 24px   | 600    | 1.4         |
| Body        | 16px   | 400    | 1.6         |
| Caption     | 14px   | 300    | 1.5         |

## 5. Imagery & Video Style

### Photography
- High contrast with cinematic color grading
- Prefer natural lighting or dramatic studio lighting
- Focus on human emotion and storytelling moments
- Behind-the-scenes shots encouraged for social media

### Video
- Cinematic aspect ratios preferred (2.39:1 or 16:9)
- Color palette should lean warm/amber or cool/teal depending on project tone
- Smooth camera movements; avoid handheld unless stylistic choice
- Include WheelWill logo sting at the beginning or end of all productions

## 6. Voice & Tone
